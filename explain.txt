
set up

1,install sass (npm i node-sass --save). because I will use scss

1,delete content of App.css and index.css
  then rename to App.scss and index.scss

2,in file index.js and App.js,
   rename to App.scss and index.scss

3, paste the google font and font awsome icon cdn into index.html
   <link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Open+Sans|Zhi+Mang+Xing&display=swap" rel="stylesheet">
   <script src="https://kit.fontawesome.com/ae35ad8e82.js" crossorigin="anonymous"></script>


4,设定index.scss。记住要将google font 的font family 放进body

    font-family: 'Open Sans', sans-serif;
    font-family: 'Zhi Mang Xing', cursive;


5，把img文件夹放入public里面。

6,写代码的时候item跟list要分开。


7.用github 账号注册netlify，建网站
